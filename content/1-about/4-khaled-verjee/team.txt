Title: Khaled Verjee

----

About: A graduate of the London School of Economics, Khaled practiced corporate securities law at White & Case in London, UK. Following that, Khaled spent a number of years in origination and structuring in the investment banking divisions of Nomura and Standard Bank of South Africa. There, Khaled focused on advising governments and corporates in Sub-Saharan Africa on issuances of debt instruments. Most recently, Khaled co-founded a record label and publishing company alongside Nettwerk Music Group and serves as a director at Dubset Media - an innovative rights management and distribution platform for derivative works.
